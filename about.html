Who are we?

We're a magazine that publishes art for ANY kids in grade K-5. Your child can get published by us, all for free! Our print issues include all art except any performance art in the form of a video. However, we include an online version of all our issues right here on our website, which does include performance videos! Want to submit to us? Click the first button on our homepage!

We started with a vision to make art fun again. In a world of YouTube and online games, we wanted to rejuvenate the love of art in children again, and now we're here! Quills & Quacks is dedicated to making art motivational by giving kids the chance to see their artwork in print!
